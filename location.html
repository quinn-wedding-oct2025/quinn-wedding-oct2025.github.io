About

The Ole Oak Barn offers the charm of a rustic landscape, while also providing enough space for all of us to celebrate together. Set on 12 acres of scenic pasture and hardwood trees, the venue features a beautifully crafted 3,200 sq ft barn made of knotty pine, along with multiple ceremony sites (including a tree-lined lawn, hardwood grove, and a covered pavilion)!

Accessibility

For guests who may need assistance getting around the venue, a golf cart and driver will be available throughout the day. If you would like to use this service, please let us know on the RSVP form under the question: “Do you have any mobility needs we should be aware of?”
